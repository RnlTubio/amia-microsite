CAPACITY BUILDING

About AMIA Program

Adaptation and Mitigation Initiative in Agriculture.

CRVA Assessment

Assessing agricultural vulnerability to climate risks using maps to guide decision-making.

NCCAG Map

Utilizing the National Color-Coded Agricultural Guide Map to identify suitable crops per region.

Climate Information

Providing climate-related data, such as Typhoon Risk maps and localized 10-day forecasts.

Advisory Portal

Integrating tools into the ACAP portal for streamlined access to advisory services.
